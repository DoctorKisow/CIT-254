In this lab, you will install two virtual machines: one with Kali Linux and the other with Metasploitable2. This exploitation environment will offer you an immersive, hands-on exploration of offensive security techniques. By utilizing the tools installed on Kali Linux and targeting the intentionally vulnerable Metasploitable2 virtual machine, you will delve into the practical realm of vulnerability exploitation. Using Metasploit's extensive library of exploits, payloads, and auxiliary modules, you will practice identifying and exploiting vulnerabilities embedded within Metasploitable2. This experience provides insight into the methodologies of ethical hackers and builds essential skills in identifying, exploiting, and securing systems within the dynamic and challenging field of cybersecurity.

OBJECTIVE
The primary objective of this lab is to empower students with the skills and knowledge required to conduct ethical and responsible penetration testing activities. By installing these tools in a virtual environment, students will gain insights into the linux operating system.
