OBJECTIVE
In this lab, you will engage with the NICE Challenge ecosystem, utilizing its virtual machines to tackle a hands-on technical challenge. The NICE Challenge Labs are designed to simulate real-world cybersecurity environments, providing you with practical experience in identifying, analyzing, and responding to security threats. Throughout this lab, you will apply skills related to system administration, threat detection, and network defense within a controlled, virtual environment.

This challenge aims to enhance your technical proficiency by requiring you to perform specific tasks that mirror scenarios you might encounter in a professional cybersecurity role. Make sure to document your observations and any solutions you implement, as this documentation will serve as a valuable reference for your continued learning and growth in cybersecurity.
